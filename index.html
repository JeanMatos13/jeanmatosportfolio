acomsa academy

Acomsa Academy, a well-established online education school, faced significant challenges with their email marketing campaigns.
 
Despite having a large subscriber base, the company struggled to convert email subscribers into paying customers.

Turning Challenges into Opportunities!

To revitalize Acomsa Academy’s email marketing, I optimized the contact list and introduced audience segmentation, allowing us to deliver more personalized and relevant content.
 
We also established automated workflows, including welcome series, cart abandonment emails, and re-engagement campaigns, to nurture leads and increase conversions.

Key Achievements

Click-Through Rate (CTR)

By renewing the content and optimizing the CTAs (calls to action), we achieved a significant increase in CTR, rising from 2% to 3.5%.

Conversion Rate  

Through strategic segmentation and personalized content, we successfully increased the conversion rate from 1.5% to a range of 2.5%.

Unsubscribe Rate

Our focus on delivering relevant and valuable content led to a reduction in the unsubscribe rate from 2.5% to a lower range of 1.5% - 2%.
